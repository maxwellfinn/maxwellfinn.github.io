Questions You Might Have

What's the time commitment?
Your Core Group meets once monthly for about 3 hours. Beyond that, it's up to you—attend city dinners, engage in Slack, join retreats. Most members say the monthly meeting is the most valuable 3 hours of their month.
Who qualifies for Hampton?
Founders and CEOs who have built a company with $1M+ in annual revenue, raised $3M+ in funding, or sold a business for $5M+. Beyond the numbers, we look for people who are generous with knowledge and committed to helping others succeed.
What's the investment?
Approximately $8,500 per year. Most members report ROI from a single conversation that far exceeds this—one member saved $2.3M from advice in his first month. The math usually works out to "one prevented mistake pays for a decade of membership."
How is Hampton different from YPO, EO, or Vistage?
Those organizations are excellent for traditional businesses. Hampton is built specifically for high-growth founders in the digital economy—SaaS, e-commerce, media, tech-enabled services. Your peers will understand the specific challenges of scaling fast in the internet age.
What if there's no chapter in my city?
We have chapters in 16+ cities and growing. If yours isn't on the list yet, you can still join and participate in virtual Core Groups, the global Slack community, and annual retreats while we build out your city.
What's the application process?
Apply online (takes 5 minutes), then have a brief call with our membership team. We review applications within 48 hours. If accepted, you'll be matched with a Core Group and can attend your first meeting within weeks.
