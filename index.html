Background

From college dorm to nine-figure results

It started at Babson College, ranked #1 for entrepreneurship. While others studied theory, I built Loot!—a mobile platform that raised millions in venture capital.

My first e-commerce brand, Startup Drugz, hit a $500K run rate in 6 months using Facebook ads when most marketers didn't even know they existed. That brand was later acquired by Russell Brunson, founder of ClickFunnels.

I then co-founded Quantum Media with Kevin Harrington—the original Shark from Shark Tank—running campaigns for Fortune 500 companies including 3M.

Today, I lead Unicorn Innovations and Unicorn Traffic, helping brands scale profitably with paid advertising on Facebook and TikTok.

"I've been in the trenches since day one. No theory—just results."
