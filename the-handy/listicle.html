The smart stroker market has exploded in the past few years, and for good reason. These devices have evolved from basic motorized sleeves into sophisticated machines that sync with content, respond to partners remotely, and deliver experiences that weren't possible even five years ago.

We evaluated each device on speed, stroke length, noise level, app functionality, content library, build quality, and overall value. Here's what we found.
