FAQ

Do both bags fit under airplane seats?
Yes, both the Bento Bag and Away Everywhere Bag fit under airplane seats on major US airlines. However, neither may fit in the official personal item sizer for airlines with stricter policies (like WestJet or Air Canada). In practice, most travelers report no issues with either bag.
Is the $100 price difference worth it?
If you travel frequently (monthly or more), yes. The Bento Bag includes a toiletry pouch, tech organizer, and shoe bags that would cost $95+ separately. Add the larger capacity, USB port, and structured design, and the value proposition favors the Bento for serious travelers. If you travel a few times a year, the Away may be sufficient.
Which bag has better organization?
The Bento Bag wins decisively on organization. Its clamshell design opens like a suitcase with dedicated compartments for everything. The Away Everywhere Bag has a single main compartment with a few internal pockets ‚Äî you'll need to buy separate packing cubes to stay organized.
Which bag looks more professional?
The Bento Bag has a more structured, professional appearance with premium vegan leather accents. It holds its shape whether full or empty. The Away Everywhere Bag has a softer, more casual look that sags when empty ‚Äî better for gym or weekend use than business travel.
What's the return policy for each bag?
Nomad Lane offers a 100-day return policy on the Bento Bag. Away offers a 100-day trial period for the Everywhere Bag. Both brands provide free returns within this window, so you can try the bag risk-free.
